\section{Future Work}
\label{sec:future_work}

The first direction for future work is expanding the Romanian dataset, especially for the racism and homophobia classes. These categories are underrepresented in the current public resources, so collecting and manually annotating more real examples would make the evaluation more reliable and reduce the instability of per-class scores.



As a conclusion of how can this be used: for example, before a post/message is posted in an online group, a post predicted as (racist/homophobic/sexist/offensive) may not be published immediately and be flagged for awaited moderator's approval. As models cannot be 100\%, a human interaction like earlier would be needed.
